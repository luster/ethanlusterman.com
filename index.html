I am a software engineer at The Hackerati, located in New York, NY. During my time here, I have worked on a number of projets and technologies, including:

  • designing an API and frontend client for a car negotiation service for buyers and dealerships
  • increasing security and writing data ingestion scripts for a marketing analytics company's Hadoop cluster
  • r&d for a replacement machine learning system for a market research company's item categorization system
  • maintaining the APIs and CMS for a top media website
  • designing a user API (Python/Django)
  • experience with Amazon Web Services (S3, EC2, EMR)
  • working with Big Data tools (Cloudera, Hadoop, Pig, Spark, Hive)
  • fluency with Machine learning theory and tools (MATLAB, Scikit-learn in Python, MLLib in Spark)
  • working extensively on fullstack web (Django, Flask, PHP)
  • working with frontend web (HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, AngularJS)

Many people have asked me over the past few years if I could make websites for their bands or personal portfolios, so I've decided to take on some part-time projects. The first site I built is for the band Time King. I worked with my friends in the band as well as with graphic artist Louis Costa. The site is static and built on GitHub pages from scratch. I also have experience putting sites into production on Amazon Web Services using frameworks such as Django and Flask.


I also recently began working with Veronica Ambrosini, a super talented artist and designer, to redo her website. This work has included a clean frontend layout that scales cleanly for iPad and iPhone, along with an easy-to-use Django-driven CMS where she can change content easily. The site is in progress, but you can check it out here!

I graduated from The Cooper Union for the Advancement of Science and Art with a B.E. in Electrical Engineering this past May, and I am currently pursuing an M.E. in Electrical Engineering. My research is focused on the intersection of machine learning, signal processing, and music. During my time at Cooper Union, I have worked on projects in music genre classification and clustering, audio dereverberation, music transcription, digital guitar effects generation, and psychoacoustic compression of audio, among others.

In my free time, I like to write, play, and record music as well as go to shows. I released my first EP, Better Off, with help from a few friends this past year. I have performed at venues and festivals such as Bowery Electric, Lit Lounge, The Vibe Lounge, and Vans Warped Tour.
